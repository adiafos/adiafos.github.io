Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Mortgage Calculator. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your data is protected.

Information We Collect

Mortgage Calculator only processes the following data, which you input for the sole purpose of calculating your mortgage:

  • Principal amount
  • Annual interest rate
  • Loan term in years

These data points are only used to perform the calculations and are not stored or transmitted in any form.

Data Usage

The data you input is used solely for the purpose of providing you with mortgage calculation results. Once the calculation is complete, the data is immediately discarded. We do not store, share, or transmit any of your data.

Cookies and Tracking

Mortgage Calculator does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies to monitor your activities. Your interaction with our application remains private and untracked.

Third-Party Services

Our application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in its privacy policy. The types of data collected by AdMob include, but are not limited to:

  • Device information (e.g., device ID, IP address)
  • Coarse location data
  • Usage data (e.g., interactions with advertisements)
  • Diagnostic information (e.g., crash logs)

This data is collected and used by AdMob to provide personalized advertising and improve its services. For more information, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement appropriate security measures to ensure that your data is protected during use. However, since no data is stored or transmitted by us, there is no risk of data breaches related to the information you provide within the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the update date will be reflected below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
